So how does it work ?

3 Simple Steps

MyCycle's process is simple: collect a cervical mucus sample, measure pH with our device, and input your BBT in the app. Our ML algorithm ensures precise results.

  • Begin by collecting a small cervical mucus sample using a silicon swab and applying it to our device. This initial step initiates the process of data collection.

  • Our device measures the pH of the cervical mucus and seamlessly transmits this vital data to our user-friendly app, where your menstrual health journey continues.

  • Enter your Basal Body Temperature (BBT) into the app, and our state-of-the-art machine learning algorithm processes the combined data to generate accurate, actionable results providing you with valuable insights into your menstrual health for confident and informed decision-making.
